About Me

It's not the mountain we conquer but ourselves.

Edmund Hillary
Hello! My name is Mia Tran, and I’m a Web Developer based in Orange County, CA.

I love learning, and I consider myself a lifelong learner. I am interested in creative design, problem solving, and product development. These interests enable me to improve the efficiency of business operations, benefit people’s lives, and place positive impacts on both businesses and individuals. Those are the reasons why I enjoy programming, and decide to pursue my career in software development.

During my last year at UC San Diego, I took an elective in Web Development, and I immediately got hooked on the subject. While working as a Data Analyst, I got an opportunity to help the Web Development team at my company developing a client portal. I was instantly captivated on the process of building an application and solving problems through programming. This was when I decided to start my career in software development.

As a Web Developer, it is rewarding to walk users through a fun and delightful experience, and to provide them with great and meaningful data. I was eager to put my creative thoughts into code, and I have truly enjoyed the process of building each and every one of my applications.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio! I am looking forward to connecting with you and getting started on new projects.

Run Buddy is a responsive web application created in a 48-hour deadline. This fitness/activity planner application lets users look up different trail details, direction, weather, and fitness events based on inputted location. I developed this application with four other developers using over four APIs.
